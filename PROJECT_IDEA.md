# Healthy Lifestyle & Food Recommendation App

## Idea Overview

Healthy Lifestyle & Food Recommendation App is designed to help users maintain a healthy lifestyle through personal health assessment, healthy food recommendations, meal planning, and health progress tracking.

---

## Main Features

### 1. Personal Health Assessment

Users provide personal information, including:

* Age
* Gender
* Height
* Weight
* Activity level
* Health goal (weight loss, muscle gain, or maintenance)

The system will:

* Calculate BMI
* Estimate daily calorie needs (TDEE)
* Recommend appropriate Protein, Carbohydrate, and Fat intake
* Provide personalized diet and exercise recommendations

---

### 2. Healthy Food Recommendation

Users can:

* Upload an ingredient image, or
* Enter a list of available ingredients

The AI system will:

* Recognize ingredients from images
* Suggest healthy recipes based on the detected ingredients

Displayed results include:

* Recipe name
* Cooking instructions
* Calories and nutritional information
* Cooking tutorial video (Optional)

---

### 3. Personalized Meal Plan

Users can:

* Select a health goal
* Choose the number of meals per day
* Specify food preferences or dietary restrictions

The system will:

* Automatically generate daily or weekly meal plans
* Balance nutrition according to the user's goals
* Allow users to edit and save meal plans

---

### 4. Progress Tracking

Users can regularly update:

* Weight
* Height
* Other body measurements

The system will:

* Track health progress over time
* Display BMI and weight trend charts
* Evaluate goal achievement progress
* Recommend adjustments to diet and exercise plans

---

### 5. Smart Notifications

The system provides reminders for:

* Drinking water
* Meal schedules
* Exercise activities
* Updating health data

Users can customize:

* Notification types
* Notification frequency

---

## Key Highlights

* Personalized health assessment based on user information.
* AI-powered ingredient recognition from images.
* Healthy recipe recommendations using available ingredients.
* Automated and personalized meal planning.
* Health progress tracking with interactive charts.
* Smart notifications to encourage healthy habits and consistency.
